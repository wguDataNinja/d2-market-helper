About Diablo 2 Resurrected Runes

As rare as they are valuable in Diablo 2 Resurrected, the small D2R items - D2R Runes allow you to obtain powerful bonuses on equipment and create powerful runewords used throughout the game. Depending on whether the runes are socketed into a helmet, weapon, armor, or shield, they grant different properties and bonuses. This makes Diablo 2 Runes one of the most versatile item types in both Ladder and Non-Ladder modes.

There are 33 different runes in Diablo 2 Resurrected, each with unique effects and rarity levels. They range from the basic El Rune to the legendary Zod Rune, one of the rarest items in the game. Higher-tier runes are required to craft many of the strongest runewords, making them highly sought after by players.

Features you should know about Diablo 2 Resurrected Runes

Much like D2R Gems, you can also combine runes with the Horadric Cube to create rarer and higher-tier runes.

Some runes are very common like El and Eld, but others like Cham and Zod are exceedingly rare, and may take thousands of hours to obtain.
